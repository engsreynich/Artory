Our Mission

At Artory, our mission is to support and empower Cambodian hand-drawing artists by connecting their original artworks with art lovers around the world. We believe in the power of creativity and culture, and we are committed to providing a platform where local talent can thrive. By making it easy to buy and sell hand-drawn art, we aim to preserve traditional skills, celebrate Cambodian identity, and ensure that artists are fairly recognized and rewarded for their work.
